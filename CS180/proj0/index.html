Task 1: Distorted Selfie

One image was taken on 1x zoom close, and another taken a few steps back with 2x zoom. It is likely that the second photo looks better than the first because when the camera is held close to my face, the center of my face is much closer to the camera, and takes up a lot more of its field of view than it does when the photographer takes a few steps back and zooms in.

Task 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

These are photos of the campanile. One was taken on 0.5x zoom close to the campanile, and the other was taken much farther from the campanile on 1x zoom. The 0.5x zoom photo looks a bit flattened compared to the 1x zoom photo.
